Our Charity Programs

With us, you can rest peacefully that your donation is going to the right places. We have several programs and projects to make use of your donation. The tribal people of Chittagong Hills Tracks, flood-affected people of Khulna District, street childs and homeless people on the roads of Chattogram and Dhaka are some groups under our chaity programs.

Food for a family

Food is our basic necessity. Without food, no human can survive long. Food gives us energy to work, to live. But there are a lot of people in the world who cannot afford to eat meals two times a day. We give them access to healthy food.

Shelter for a family

Thousands of people in our country live under the poverty line. Most of them has no home to live in. They usually live in the footpath of busy streets or in a slum with lots of people. We provide them a place to live in comfortably.

Education for a child

It is said that education is the backbone of a nation. But hundreds of street childs in our country has no access to quality education. Because of that they cannot contribute to the society. We have evening school programs to help these children with their education.

Help the people of Dinajpur

This year, drought hit the people of Dinajpur pretty hard. The farmers could not produce necessary crops to feed their needs due to the lack of water.
